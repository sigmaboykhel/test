Sigma Boy Speech about War

War, the result of humanity’s greatest failures, where lives are lost, dreams are destroyed, and the pain lingers far beyond the fighting. It is the heavy cost of failing to choose peace. War is destruction, pain, and loss—it tears families apart and leaves nations in ruins. It is not bravery or honor; it is a cycle of hatred that feeds on innocent lives. The sound of bombs replaces laughter, and the cries of the wounded drown out hope. What do we gain from war? Broken homes, grieving hearts, and a future robbed of peace. Weapons do not solve problems; they only create scars that last for generations. We must rise above personal grievances and fight for the freedom and future of our nation. We have the power to choose understanding over violence, unity over division. Let us not let greed, anger, or power blind us to the value of human life. War may be fought with guns, but peace is built with compassion and love. The world does not need more battles—it needs more people willing to fight for peace. Mabuhay ang Pilipinas!

Thank you for listening!
